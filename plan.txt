Plan for Attendance Script

Purpose:
	The objective of this script is to automate the functions of the Admin for tracking attendance. The actions that will
	be automated will be Personal Days (PB), Diarrhea and Vomitting (DV), and No Call No Show (NCNS). 
	
Objective:
	When a callout occurs the students can be recorded as absent for whatever reason and it will be recorded.
	
Future Goals:
	Be able to track late notices
	Be able to automate printing Memos and Write ups for attendance

	
Notes:
	Should be able to interface with a gui. components should be
		name (order?)
		reason (late, pb, dv, ncns)
